Era 1 — Biome Selection

Choose your dungeon's starting biome before selecting your race. Each biome grants starting resources, persistent run modifiers, and affects how well different creature types perform throughout the run. Biome selection is not yet functional — this tab is a planning reference for review and expansion.

Creature Suitability Scale

Each creature has a preferred biome (▲) and a difficult biome (▼). When a biome is active, all creatures display a suitability badge on their selection card. Future implementation will apply the corresponding production and growth bonuses/penalties below.

Excellent Native environment — +15% to all production · +15% to population growth rate
Good Comfortable terrain — +8% to all production · +5% to population growth rate
Neutral No preference — standard production and growth, no bonus or penalty
Challenging Adaptation required — −10% to all production · −10% to population growth rate
Hostile Severe penalties — −25% to all production · −20% growth · biome hardship modifier active

Dungeon Keeper v0.21 — You are the keeper of a fledgling dungeon. Gather resources, house creatures, and expand your underground empire. Keep your creatures fed or watch them desert you — or worse.

Build a Lair to unlock housing, stockpile food to attract creatures, then assign them to buildings. Each Farm worker produces 2 food/s but every creature eats 1 food/s. Expand carefully — starvation kills.
