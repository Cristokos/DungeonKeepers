DungeonKeepers

Deep beneath the Material Plane, where light has never reached and silence is eternal, something stirred. Not a creature or a god, but older — a pocket of raw arcane will woven into the world itself. A Dungeon Mind, newly conscious and hungry to become something more.

You are that mind. You have no body, no voice, no name — only stone, shadow, and the faint pulse of eldritch magic humming from the Underdark below. Attract creatures that resonate with your nature: goblins, dragons, undead, fey, or something stranger still. Build your chambers, research the arcane arts, and grow in power before adventurers find you.

Choose your inhabitants wisely. In time, what lives inside you becomes you. Grow strong enough, and you will venture beyond your stone walls to challenge other Dungeons across the planes. Evolve, conquer, or fall into obscurity — forgotten by the ages like so many dungeons before you.
